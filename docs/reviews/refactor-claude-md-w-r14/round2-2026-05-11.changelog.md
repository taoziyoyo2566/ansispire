It applies to every rule/config edit (this file included), so it's behavioural protocol, not a stand-alone "feedback & evolution" topic. Folding into §0 makes the rule fire at the moment it matters (when editing) rather than living in a section a reader might skip.

The `ai-workflow.md §1` pointer collapses to a single footer line under the table — readers who want the long form know where to go; readers who just need the entry decision (which tier am I?) get it without leaving the file.
