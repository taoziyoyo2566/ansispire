## Intent
To fulfill the project's vision as a "management control system" by providing an autonomous feedback loop. The reactor transforms passive audit events into active responses, enabling self-healing and instant alerting.

## Explicitly NOT Done
- Implementation of a complex expression parser (kept simple key-value matching for stability).
- Deployment of a full Java-based `ansible-rulebook` environment.

### 3. Functional Design
The reactor uses a non-blocking `f.readline()` with a configurable `POLL_INTERVAL`, ensuring minimal CPU impact while maintaining near real-time response capability.
